## Critical Character Issues Validated

### Aaron - CRITICAL OMISSION CONFIRMED
**Source Material Check:** SEOUL_IDENTITY_v3.pdf does not include Aaron either. This appears to be a conceptual gap from the original development phase. The logline materials reference Aaron as "American operative who turns out to be false ally" but he was never written.

**Impact Assessment:**
- Missing American-to-American betrayal raises stakes
- No mirror character showing what Jake could become
- Reduced "who can I trust" paranoia
- Weakened antagonist presence

**Resolution Required:** Aaron must be created and integrated into screenplay.

### Mr. Kim - ABANDONMENT CONFIRMED
**Last Appearance:** Scene 7, Line 308 (approximately page 10)
**Scenes After:** 57 scenes without Mr. Kim

**Impact Assessment:**
- Handler/antagonist disappears with no explanation
- No confrontation when Jake learns truth
- Agent Park fills role awkwardly as stranger
- Violates promise to audience from strong introduction

**Resolution Required:** Mr. Kim must return for Act Three confrontation.
